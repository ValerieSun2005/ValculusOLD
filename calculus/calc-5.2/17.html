5.2 Exercises — Problem 17

\((17)\) Evaluate \(\displaystyle \int \cos(11x) \cos(2x) \, \textrm{d} x \).

We can rewrite the integral as $$ \int \frac{1}{2}\left[\cos(11x + 2x) + \cos(11x - 2x) \right] \, \textrm{d} x $$ $$ = \int \frac{1}{2}\left[\cos(13x) + \cos(9x) \right] \, \textrm{d} x $$ $$ = \bbox[border: 2px solid white, 2pt]{\frac{1}{26} \sin(13x) + \frac{1}{18} \sin(9x) + C.} $$
