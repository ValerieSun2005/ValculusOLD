5.2 Exercises — Problem 18

\((18)\) Evaluate \(\displaystyle \int \sin(13x) \sin(9x) \, \textrm{d} x \).

We can rewrite the integral as $$ \int \frac{1}{2}\left[\cos(13x - 9x) - \cos(13x + 9x) \right] \, \textrm{d} x $$ $$ = \int \frac{1}{2}\left[\cos(4x) - \cos(22x) \right] \, \textrm{d} x $$ $$ = \bbox[border: 2px solid white, 2pt]{\frac{1}{8} \sin(4x) - \frac{1}{44} \sin(22x) + C.} $$
