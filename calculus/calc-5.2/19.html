5.2 Exercises — Problem 19

\((19)\) Evaluate \(\displaystyle \int \cos(11x) \sin(3x) \, \textrm{d} x \).

We can rewrite the integral as $$ \int \frac{1}{2}\left[\sin(11x + 3x) - \sin(11x - 3x) \right] \, \textrm{d} x $$ $$ = \int \frac{1}{2}\left[\sin(14x) - \sin(8x) \right] \, \textrm{d} x $$ $$ = \bbox[border: 2px solid white, 2pt]{-\frac{1}{28} \cos(14x) + \frac{1}{16} \cos(8x) + C.} $$
