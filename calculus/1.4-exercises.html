\(3.\)

Determine whether \(3^{x + 4}\) is continuous at \(x = 2\).


\(5.\) Let \( f(x)= \begin{cases} x^2 - 3x + 8 &x \lt -1 \\[2ex] \sin(x + 1) &x \geq -1. \end{cases} \)

Determine whether \(f(x)\) is continuous at \(x = -1\).


\(7.\) Let \( g(x)= \begin{cases} 3^{\sqrt{8 - x}} &x \leq 4 \\[2ex] x^2 - 3x + 4 &x \gt 4. \end{cases} \)

Determine whether \(g(x)\) is continuous at \(x = 4\).


\(9.\) Let \( g(x)= \begin{cases} k^2 - 4x &x \leq 2 \\[2ex] kx &x \gt 2. \end{cases} \)

Find the values of \(k\) for which \(g(x)\) is continuous at \(x = 2\).


\(11.\) Let \( k(x)= \begin{cases} 6 - x &x \leq p \\[2ex] x^2 + 4 &x \gt p. \end{cases} \)

Find the values of \(p\) for which \(k(x)\) is continuous at \(x = p\).

\(4.\)

Determine whether \(\sqrt{2 - 4x}\) is continuous at \(x = 1/2\).


\(6.\) Let \( v(x)= \begin{cases} \sqrt{1 - x} &x \leq 1 \\[2ex] \log_4(x) &x \gt 1. \end{cases} \)

Determine whether \(v(x)\) is continuous at \(x = 1\).


\(8.\) Let \( h(x)= \begin{cases} \sin(x) &x \lt \pi/4 \\[2ex] \cos(x) &x \gt \pi/4. \end{cases} \)

Determine whether \(h(x)\) is continuous at \(x = \pi/4\).


\(10.\) Let \( f(x)= \begin{cases} 8k - x^2 &x \lt 4 \\[2ex] k^2 \sqrt{x} - k^3 &x \geq 4. \end{cases} \)

Find the values of \(k\) for which \(f(x)\) is continuous at \(x = 4\).


\(12.\) Let \( k(x)= \begin{cases} e^{2x} + 7 &x \leq q \\[2ex] 5e^x + 1 &x \gt q. \end{cases} \)

Find the values of \(q\) for which \(k(x)\) is continuous at \(x = q\).

\(13.\)

Let \( f(x)= \begin{cases} px + q &x \leq 2 \\[2ex] px^2 - 2q &x \gt 2. \end{cases} \) It is known that \(f(x)\) is continuous at \(x = 2\) and \(f(1) = 4\). What are \(p\) and \(q\) ?


\(14.\)

Let \( g(x)= \begin{cases} ax^2 - bx &x \leq 1 \\[2ex] cx + d &x \gt 1. \end{cases} \) It is known that \(g(x)\) is continuous at \(x = 1\), \(f(-1) = -3\), \(f(2) = 0\), and \(f(3) = 1\). What are \(a, b, c,\) and \(d\) ?
