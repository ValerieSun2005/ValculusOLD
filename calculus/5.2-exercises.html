5.2 Exercises — Trigonometric Integrals

For exercises 1–20, evaluate the following integrals.

\(1.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \sin(x) \cos^2(x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(2.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \sin^3(x) \cos^4(x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(3.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \sin^2(x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(4.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \cos^3(x) \sin^4(x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(5.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \sec^2(x) \tan(x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(6.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \sec^3(x) \tan^3(x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(7.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \sin^5(x) \sec^6(x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(8.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \csc^4(x) \cot(x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(9.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \csc^8(x) \cot^2(x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(10.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \cot^3(x) \csc^4(x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(11.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \sec^5(x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(12.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \sin^4(x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(13.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \csc^3(x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(14.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \tan^3(x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(15.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \sec^4(x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(16.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \tan^4(x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(17.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \cos(11x) \cos(2x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(18.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \sin(13x) \sin(9x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(19.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \cos(11x) \sin(3x) \, \textrm{d} x \)

\(20.\)
\(\displaystyle \int \left( \sin(x)\cos^2(8x) - \sin^2(8x) \sin(x) \right) \, \textrm{d} x \)
