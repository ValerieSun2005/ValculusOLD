Welcome to Calculus. The following sections are designed for all calculus students, including students taking AP®︎ Calculus and the college courses Calculus I and Calculus II. Each section provides an overview of the concept and contains exercises and solutions. PDFs of the notes, exercises, and solutions are also presented in the sections. There are supplemental resources⁠—⁠such as cheat sheets, Quizlets, and practice tests⁠—⁠included in each chapter, so feel free to use all of these tools as needed.
