Problem 27 — 5.1 Exercises (Integration by Parts)

Evaluate \(\displaystyle \int \ppkn^4 \ln(\ppkn) \left(5 \sin(3 \ln \ppkn) + 3 \cos (3 \ln \ppkn)\right) \, \dd \ppkn.\)

SOLUTION First, we rewrite the integral as \[\int \frac{\ppkn^5}{\ppkn} \ln(\ppkn) \left(5 \sin(3 \ln \ppkn) + 3 \cos (3 \ln \ppkn)\right) \, \dd \ppkn.\] Now, we will perform the substitution \(k = \ln(\ppkn)\) because then \(\displaystyle \dd k = \frac{1}{\ppkn} \, \dd \ppkn\) and \(\ppkn = e^k.\) The integral then becomes \[\int k e^{5k} \left(5 \sin(3k) + 3 \cos(3k) \right) \, \dd k.\] At this point, we will use integration by parts. Referencing the formula, \[\int u \, \dd v = uv - \int v \, \dd u,\] we let \(u = k\) and \(\dd v = e^{5k} \left(5 \sin(3k) + 3 \cos(3k) \right) \, \dd k.\) Notice that \[\frac{\dd}{\dd k} \left(e^{5k} \sin(3k) \right) = e^{5k} \left( 5\sin(3k) + 3 \cos(3k) \right). \] Therefore, \(\dd u = \dd k\) and \(v = e^{5k} \sin(3k)\) (reversing the product rule). Plugging in these expressions gives \[ \int k e^{5k} \left(5 \sin(3k) + 3 \cos(3k) \right) \, \dd k = ke^{5k} \sin(3k) - \int e^{5k} \sin(3k) \, \dd k. \] In order to evaluate \(\displaystyle \int e^{5k} \sin(3k) \, \dd k,\) we can perform tabular integration.



Thus, we have \[ \begin{aligned} \int e^{5k} \sin(3k) \, \dd k &= \frac{1}{5} e^{5k} \sin(3k) - \frac{3}{25} e^{5k} \cos(3k) - \frac{9}{25} \int e^{5k} \sin(3k) \, \dd k \\ \frac{34}{25} \int e^{5k} \sin(3k) \, \dd k &= \frac{1}{5} e^{5k} \sin(3k) - \frac{3}{25} e^{5k} \cos(3k) + C \\ \int e^{5k} \sin(3k) \, \dd k &= \frac{5}{34} e^{5k} \sin(3k) - \frac{3}{34} e^{5k} \cos(3k) + C. \\ \end{aligned} \] Plugging in this expression produces \[ \begin{aligned} \int k e^{5k} \left(5 \sin(3k) + 3 \cos(3k) \right) \, \dd k &= ke^{5k} \sin(3k) - \int e^{5k} \sin(3k) \, \dd k \\ &= ke^{5k} \sin(3k) - \left(\frac{5}{34} e^{5k} \sin(3k) - \frac{3}{34} e^{5k} \cos(3k)\right) + C \\ &= ke^{5k} \sin(3k) + \frac{3}{34} e^{5k} \cos(3k) - \frac{5}{34} e^{5k} \sin(3k) + C \\ &= \box{\ln(\ppkn) \ppkn^5 \sin(3 \ln \ppkn) + \frac{3}{34} \ppkn^5 \cos(3 \ln \ppkn) - \frac{5}{34} \ppkn^5 \sin(3 \ln \ppkn) + C.} \end{aligned} \\ \]
