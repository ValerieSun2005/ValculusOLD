Problem 15 — 5.1 Exercises (Integration by Parts)

Evaluate \(\displaystyle \int (2x - 4) \left(x^2 - 4x + 7\right)^2 \left(2x^2 - 8x + 5 \right)^4 \, \dd x. \)

SOLUTION We should first perform the substitution \(k = x^2 - 4x + 7\) because then \(\dd k = (2x - 4) \, \dd x\) and the integral becomes \[\int k^2 \left(2k - 9\right)^4 \, \dd k. \] Performing tabular integration, we choose to differentiate \(k^2\) and integrate \((2k - 9)^4.\)

Pairing the expressions on either side of each arrow, being cautious of the signs, produce \[ \begin{aligned} \frac{1}{10}k^2(2k - 9)^5 - \frac{1}{60}k(2k - 9)^6 + \frac{1}{840}(2k - 9)^7 + C \\ =\bbox[border: 2px solid white, 2pt] { \frac{1}{10}(x^2 - 4x + 7)^2(2(x^2 - 4x + 7) - 9)^5 - \frac{1}{60}(x^2 - 4x + 7)(2(x^2 - 4x + 7) - 9)^6 + \frac{1}{840}(2(x^2 - 4x + 7) - 9)^7 + C. } \end{aligned} \]
