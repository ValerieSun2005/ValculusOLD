Problem 14 — 5.1 Exercises (Integration by Parts)

Evaluate \(\displaystyle \int \left(3t - 5\right)^3 \left(t + 9\right)^8 \, \dd t. \)

SOLUTION Rather than expanding the binomials (which would probably take several hours!), we can instead utilize tabular integration (or three iterations of integration by parts) by choosing to differentiate \((3t - 5)^3\) and integrate \((t + 9)^8\) (the other way around also works; however, choosing to differentiate \((t + 9)^8\) would require eight iterations rather than three.)



Pairing the expressions on either side of each arrow (and being cautious of each assigned sign), we get \[ \bbox[border: 2px solid white, 2pt] { \frac{1}{9}(3t - 5)^3 (t + 9)^9 - \frac{9}{90} (3t - 5)^2 (t + 9)^{10} + \frac{54}{990}(3t - 5)(t + 9)^{11} - \frac{162}{11880}(t + 9)^{12} + C. } \]
