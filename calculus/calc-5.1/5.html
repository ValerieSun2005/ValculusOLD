Problem 5 — 5.1 Exercises (Integration by Parts)

Evaluate \(\displaystyle \int t^4 \ln(t) \, \dd t. \)

SOLUTION We can utilize the formula for integration by parts, \[\int u \, \dd v = uv - \int v \, \dd u, \] to re-express the integral. We will choose \(u = \ln(t)\) and \(\dd v = t^4 \, \dd t.\) Then, \(\displaystyle \dd u = \frac{1}{t} \, \dd t \) and \(\displaystyle v = \frac{1}{5} t^5. \) We then have \[ \begin{aligned} \int t^4 \ln(t) \, \dd t &= \frac{1}{5}t^5 \ln(t) - \int \left(\frac{1}{5} t^5\right) \left(\frac{1}{t} \, \dd t\right) \\ &= \frac{1}{5}t^5 \ln(t) - \frac{1}{5} \int t^4 \, \dd t \\ &= \bbox[border: 2px solid white, 2pt]{\frac{1}{5}t^5 \ln(t) - \frac{1}{25} t^5 + C.} \end{aligned} \]
