5.5 Exercises — Problem 16

\((16)\) Determine whether \(\displaystyle \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} \, \textrm{d} x \) is a proper or improper integral. If it is improper, classify the type (i.e, Type I or Type II).

Because at least one bound of the integral is infinite, the integral is improper (Type I).
